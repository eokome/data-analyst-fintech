## Company Overview

EPCVIP is a lead-generation service specializing in the financial services sector. They position themselves as industry frontrunners in FinTech, emphasizing their ability to deliver qualified prospects and measurable outcomes.

The company is headquartered in Calabasas, California, with approximately $5.6 million in revenue and 75 employees. Founder and CEO: Valeriy Zeltser.

## Core Services

- **Lead Generation** — Producing tens of thousands of leads daily
- **Campaign Management** — PPC, search engine, display, and email marketing
- **Publisher Network** — Connecting content creators with financial advertisers
- **Advertiser Network** — Matching financial companies with qualified prospects

## Value Proposition

The company emphasizes three primary strengths:

1. **Performance Metrics** — Claims to deliver "the highest EPCs in the industry" (earnings per click)
2. **Quality Focus** — "We strive to build a program that doesn't just drive traffic, it drives high-quality leads, sales, and most importantly, results."
3. **Technology Advantage** — Proprietary platform offering real-time analytics, tracking, and performance optimization

## Proprietary Platform Features

- Dynamic analytics dashboard for publishers
- Data optimization and list segmentation for advertisers
- Real-time campaign performance monitoring
- Customizable landing pages
- Gross revenue and conversion rate tracking
- Enterprise-grade data security with encryption

## Industries Served

Financial Services exclusively — the company's entire business model centers on connecting financial product advertisers with consumers actively seeking financial products.

## Business Model

EPCVIP operates a performance-based lead generation network. Publishers drive traffic; advertisers pay for qualified leads. The platform optimizes across channels (PPC, search, display, email) to maximize earnings per click for publishers while delivering conversion-ready prospects to financial advertisers.
