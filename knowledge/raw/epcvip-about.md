## Company Profile

EPCVIP, Inc. is a technology-driven financial services lead generation company based in Calabasas, California. The company specializes exclusively in the financial services vertical, connecting consumers with financial product offers across lending, credit, and insurance categories.

## Key Facts

- **Founded:** Active as of at least 2018 (profile per LinkedIn and Crunchbase)
- **CEO/Founder:** Valeriy Zeltser
- **Employees:** ~75
- **Revenue:** ~$5.6 million
- **Location:** Calabasas, CA
- **Model:** Performance marketing / affiliate lead generation

## Core Competencies

EPCVIP has developed a proprietary, customizable lead optimization platform specifically designed for the financial services industry. The platform enables:

- Banks and financial institutions to access qualified consumer leads
- Publishers to monetize financial content at the "highest EPCs in the industry"
- Real-time analytics and performance optimization
- List segmentation and data-driven targeting

## Marketing Expertise

The company possesses deep expertise in:
- Pay-per-click (PPC) advertising
- Search engine marketing
- Display advertising
- Email marketing

These channels collectively generate tens of thousands of leads per day across financial product categories.

## Industry Recognition

EPCVIP was named a Top Featured Vendor in Financial Services Review (2022), cited for its proprietary technology and performance results in the financial lead generation space.

## Strategic Relevance

EPCVIP's business model is directly affected by consumer demand signals in financial products. When consumers face problems with financial products (evidenced by CFPB complaint spikes), they actively seek alternatives — making complaint data a high-value targeting signal for EPCVIP's advertiser network.
